Community Center Bundles

Completing the Community Center restores Pelican Town and unlocks valuable areas. Each room requires multiple bundles to be fulfilled. Plan ahead so you don't miss seasonal items!

Bundle Tips

  • Check what you need each season BEFORE it starts
  • The Traveling Cart (Fri/Sun in Cindersap Forest) sells rare bundle items
  • Quality doesn't matter for bundles - base quality works fine
  • Some bundles only need a subset of items (e.g., 4 of 6)
  • Completing all bundles triggers a cutscene and unlocks community upgrades

Completion Strategy

  • Year 1 Priority: Pantry (Greenhouse) and Vault (Desert access)
  • Hardest Items: Red Cabbage (Year 2+ at Pierre's or Traveling Cart), Rabbit's Foot, Truffle
  • Don't Miss: Catfish (Spring rain only), Sunfish (Spring/Summer, sunny only)
  • Traveling Cart: Check every Friday and Sunday for rare items
  • Animal Planning: Buy barn animals early for Large Milk/Duck Egg by Year 1
