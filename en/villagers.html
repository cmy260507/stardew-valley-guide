Villager Gifts Guide

Building relationships with villagers is a core part of Stardew Valley. Give loved gifts on birthdays for massive friendship boosts. Here are all 12 romance candidates with their favorite gifts.

Gift Giving Tips

  • You can give 2 gifts per villager per week (resets Sunday)
  • Birthday gifts give 8x the normal friendship points
  • Loved gifts give 80 points (+20 with gift taste modifier)
  • Universal loves: Golden Pumpkin, Magic Rock Candy, Pearl, Prismatic Shard, Rabbit's Foot
  • At 8 hearts, give a Bouquet (from Pierre's) to start dating
  • At 10 hearts, give a Mermaid's Pendant (from Old Mariner) to propose

Friendship Milestones

  • 2 Hearts: Receive recipes in the mail from villagers
  • 3 Hearts: Can enter villager bedrooms
  • 5 Hearts: First unique cutscene for most villagers
  • 8 Hearts: Give Bouquet to start dating (romance candidates)
  • 10 Hearts: Give Mermaid's Pendant to propose marriage
  • 14 Hearts: Final spouse event after marriage
