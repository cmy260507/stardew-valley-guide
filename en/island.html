Ginger Island Guide

Ginger Island is the endgame area introduced in version 1.5. It features year-round summer growing, the Volcano Dungeon, the weapon Forge, and 130 Golden Walnuts to find. Access requires completing the Community Center and repairing Willy's boat.

Getting to Ginger Island

  • Complete the Community Center (or Joja equivalent)
  • Visit Willy's shop - he reveals a back room with a broken boat
  • Repair the boat: 200 Hardwood + 5 Iridium Bars + 5 Battery Packs
  • Boat departs daily at Willy's shop; free travel once repaired
  • Island has permanent Summer weather - crops never die here

Golden Walnuts

Overview (130 Total)

  • Golden Walnuts are the currency for unlocking island features
  • Found by: digging, fishing, harvesting crops, solving puzzles, combat
  • Leo's parrot gives hints for remaining walnut locations
  • Key Unlocks:
  • 10 Walnuts - Farm Area (grow crops year-round)
  • 10 Walnuts - Parrot Express (fast travel around island)
  • 20 Walnuts - Resort (villagers visit the island)
  • 20 Walnuts - Trader unlocks (Island Trader inventory)
  • 100 Walnuts - Mr. Qi's Walnut Room (endgame challenges)

Volcano Dungeon

Volcano Overview

  • 10 floors of increasingly difficult combat and puzzles
  • Monsters: Lava Lurks, Hot Heads, Tiger Slimes, Magma Sprites, Magma Sparkers, False Magma Caps
  • Contains Cinder Shard nodes (used for crafting and the Forge)
  • Treasure chests on each floor have unique loot
  • Floor 5 has a shortcut unlock (pour water on lava with watering can)
  • Floor 10 has the Forge
  • Dragon Teeth drop from Lava Lurks (needed for crafting)

Best Forge Enchantments

  • Weapons: Crusader (bonus vs undead/mummies), Vampiric (heal on kill)
  • Pickaxe: Swift (faster swing), Powerful (more geodes/resources)
  • Watering Can: Reaching (larger area)
  • Hoe: Reaching (larger tilling area)
  • Axe: Shaving (extra wood from trees)
  • Best Combined Ring: Iridium Band + Burglar's Ring (or Napalm Ring for mining)

Mr. Qi's Walnut Room

Endgame Challenges (100 Walnuts Required)

  • Special orders rotate weekly - earn Qi Gems as currency
  • Qi's Crop: Grow 500 Qi Fruit (Qi Gems reward)
  • Qi's Hungry Challenge: Reach Skull Cavern floor 100 without eating
  • Qi's Kindness: Give 50 loved gifts in a week
  • Qi Gem Shop: Buy Junimo Chest, Hopper, Horse Flute, Enricher, Pressure Nozzle, Key to the Town
  • Perfection Tracker: Shows completion % on the wall
