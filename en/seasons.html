Seasonal Crops Guide

Choosing the right crops is key to making money in Stardew Valley. This guide covers the best crops for each season with profit analysis.

Spring (Day 1 - Day 28)

CropSeed CostGrowthSell PriceProfit/DayNotes
Strawberry100g (Egg Fest)8 days120gHighBuy at Egg Festival (13th), regrows
Cauliflower80g12 days175g7.9gGiant crop chance, high early profit
Potato50g6 days80g5gChance for extra harvest
Garlic40g4 days60g5gFast growth for quick cash
Kale70g6 days110g6.7gGood value
Parsnip20g4 days35g3.75gStarting seeds, get going early

Spring Tips

  • Plant your 15 free parsnip seeds on Day 1 immediately
  • Buy potatoes and cauliflower seeds ASAP
  • Save money for Strawberry seeds at Egg Festival (Day 13)
  • Keep one cauliflower for the Community Center bundle

  • After unlocking Desert, buy Starfruit seeds first
  • Plant melons in 3x3 for giant crop chance
  • Hops + Kegs = continuous Pale Ale income

  • Cranberries are the best regrow crop in fall
  • Pumpkins in 3x3 can become giant crops
  • If you have Ancient Seeds, plant Day 1 for harvest by season end
  • Don't forget Community Center bundle crops

Winter (Day 1 - Day 28)

No outdoor planting in Winter!

  • Greenhouse - Unlocked via Pantry bundles, grow year-round
  • Foraging - Crystal Fruit, Snow Yam, Winter Root, Crocus
  • Mining - Use free time to push deep into mines
  • Fishing - Unique winter fish, good prices
  • Tool Upgrades - No crops = perfect time to upgrade
