Mining Guide

The Mines are essential for gathering ores, gems, and combat resources. Unlock new areas as you progress deeper. This guide covers all 120 floors plus advanced mining tips.

Mining Basics

  • The Mines are located northeast of the Carpenter's Shop
  • Open from 6am; if you pass out at 2am you lose items and gold
  • Every 5 floors has an elevator checkpoint (can skip back later)
  • Upgrade your pickaxe at Clint's for faster ore breaking
  • Bring food for energy and health recovery
  • Ladders appear when you kill all monsters or break rocks

Mine Floor Levels

Floors 1-39 (Earth)

Ore: Copper Ore

Gems: Amethyst, Topaz, Earth Crystal

Monsters: Green Slimes, Bugs, Rock Crabs, Grubs

Tips: Easy floors, use basic pickaxe. Craft Cherry Bombs to clear rocks quickly. Bugs drop Bug Meat for bait.

Floors 40-79 (Ice)

Ore: Iron Ore, Copper Ore

Gems: Aquamarine, Frozen Tear, Jade

Monsters: Frost Bats, Dust Sprites, Ghosts

Tips: Dust Sprites drop Coal (kill 500 for Burglar's Ring). Frozen Tears are loved by Sebastian. Iron is needed for Quality Sprinklers.

Floors 80-119 (Lava)

Ore: Gold Ore, Iron Ore

Gems: Ruby, Fire Quartz, Diamond (rare)

Monsters: Shadow Brutes, Shadow Shamans, Lava Bats, Metal Heads, Squid Kids

Tips: Significantly harder, bring lots of food. Gold Ore is needed for Quality Sprinklers and crafting. Shadow Brutes hit hard.

Floor 120 (Bottom)

Reward: Skull Key

Unlocks: Skull Cavern in the Desert

Tips: Reaching floor 120 is a major milestone. The Skull Key opens the door in the Desert mines for Iridium Ore.

Key Milestones

Floor 5: First elevator checkpoint
Floor 20: Steady copper supply established
Floor 40: Ice levels begin, iron ore appears
Floor 60: Frozen Tear and Jade nodes appear
Floor 80: Lava levels begin, gold ore appears
Floor 100: Stardrop reward (first time reaching)
Floor 120: Bottom reached - receive Skull Key

Advanced Mining Tips

  • Use staircases (craft from 99 Stone) to skip difficult or spiral floors
  • Bring Bombs and Mega Bombs to clear floors fast
  • Equip boots with good defense and immunity stats
  • The Galaxy Sword (from Desert) is the best pre-Volcano weapon
  • On lucky days (check TV fortune), you find more ladders and gem nodes
  • Jade in Crystalariums can be traded for Staircases from the Desert Trader (Sundays)
  • Spicy Eel (+1 Speed, +1 Luck) is ideal mine food - buy from Desert Trader or get from Serpents
